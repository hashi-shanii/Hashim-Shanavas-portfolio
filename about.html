About

Creative and driven Game Developer with a passion for crafting immersive gaming experiences. Proficient in C# programming and skilled in Unity game development, including 2D and 3D projects. Experienced designing and implementing engaging gameplay mechanics, polished user interfaces, and interactive environments. Known for attention to detail and the ability to translate concepts into functional, visually appealing game elements. We are dedicated to learning and utilizing the latest tools and techniques in Unity and game design to deliver exceptional user experiences.

Why is all of this important?

As a generalist, I’m quick to learn, adapt, and leverage all of that experience to mold whatever we are making into something fantastic. My varied experiences also help me see the bigger picture, see how things can be put together, and often anticipate roadblocks before we hit them so we can course correct in time. This wide variety of skills and experiences also help make me a better leader and mentor. I’ve been a discipline lead as well as a game director multiple times and received great feedback from my peers. I’ve also mentored other designers and helped guide several to fulfilling career goals. As a leader, I strive to be empathetic and serve those I lead to help us all achieve greatness. I love learning new things, growing, and pouring my passion into making games. I hope to keep doing it for many years to come. Outside of making games, I love playing trumpet & flugelhorn, cooking, coffee, tea, cats, travel, cycling, comics, going to Comic Cons, board games, and of course, video games!

Yours, KS Muhammed Hashim
